Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Tailo ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Tailo is designed to minimize data collection. Most data is stored locally on your device.

However, we may collect certain information through third-party services:

  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, usage data, and other information for personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Purchase Information: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. Purchase-related data (such as subscription status) may be processed.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Provide and improve the app experience
  • Display ads
  • Manage subscriptions and premium features

Data Storage

Most user data (such as pet information, user profiles, and logs) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob
  • RevenueCat

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data according to their terms.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. All data transmission (if any) is encrypted using secure protocols (HTTPS). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Account and Data Deletion

Users can delete their account and all associated data directly within the app (if such feature is available).

Since most data is stored locally on the user's device, deleting the app or clearing app data will permanently remove all user information.

If you need assistance with data deletion, you can contact us via email.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to stop using the app at any time. You may delete your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
