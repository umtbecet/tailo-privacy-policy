Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Tailo ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Tailo is designed to minimize unnecessary data collection. Some app data is stored locally on your device, and some account and pet care data may be stored on our server when you create an account, sign in, sync data, or use pet sharing features.

We may collect or process the following information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may store your email address, name, preferred language, authentication details, and account identifiers.
  • Pet Care Data: Pet profiles, care routines, vaccinations, medication records, weight records, symptoms, veterinary visit records, and related structured care data may be synced to our server.
  • Pet Sharing Data: If you share a pet with another user, we may store the pet sharing relationship, shared email address, role, and access status.
  • Local Media: Photos and videos are currently stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our server.
  • Advertising Data: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers, usage data, and other information for personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Purchase Information: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. Purchase-related data, such as subscription status, may be processed.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Provide and improve the app experience
  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Sync pet care data across devices
  • Enable pet sharing between users
  • Display ads
  • Manage subscriptions and premium features
  • Maintain app security and prevent misuse

Data Storage

Tailo uses a local-first design. Data is first stored on your device so the app can remain useful and responsive.

When you create an account, sign in, sync data, or use sharing features, supported structured data may also be stored on our server using PocketBase. This may include account data, pet profiles, pet care records, health records, and sharing permissions.

Photos and videos are currently not uploaded to our server. They remain on your device unless a future version clearly adds a cloud media feature.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob
  • RevenueCat
  • PocketBase server infrastructure for account, sync, and sharing features

These services have their own privacy policies and may collect data according to their terms.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. Data transmission between the app and server should use secure protocols whenever available. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Account and Data Deletion

Users can delete their Tailo account and associated synced data directly within the app.

To delete your account in the app:

  1. Open Tailo and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm the deletion dialog.

When you delete your account from the app, Tailo deletes your cloud account, synced pet data stored on the server, pet sharing records owned by your account, and Tailo account data stored on the device.

Photos and videos are currently not uploaded to our server. Local media files stored only on your device may be removed by deleting your account in the app, clearing app data, or uninstalling the app.

If you cannot access the app and need account deletion assistance, contact us from the email address used for your Tailo account and include the subject line "Tailo account deletion request".

Contact email for deletion requests: bctumt@gmail.com

We may ask you to confirm that you control the account email before deleting the account. Account deletion is processed as soon as technically possible. Some limited backup, security, fraud prevention, legal, or operational records may be retained only when required and then removed according to normal retention schedules.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not specifically directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Rights

You have the right to stop using the app at any time. You may delete local data by deleting your account in the app, uninstalling the app, or clearing app storage.

You may also request assistance with account and data deletion by contacting us at the email address listed below.
