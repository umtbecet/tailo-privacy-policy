Tailo is designed to minimize data collection. Most data is stored locally on your device.

  • Purchase Information: We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. Purchase-related data (such as subscription status) may be processed.

How We Use Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Display ads
  • Manage subscriptions and premium features

Data Storage

Most user data (such as pet information and logs) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Children’s Privacy

Our app is not specifically directed to children under 13.
